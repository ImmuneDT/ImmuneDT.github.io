Welcome to the Building Immune Digital Twins (BIDT) initiative ‚Äî a global, community-driven effort to develop computational replicas of the human immune system. Our goal is to advance our understanding and treatment of diseases by simulating immune responses tailored to individuals.

<p>Whether you're a researcher, clinician, or data scientist, join us in shaping the future of personalized medicine.</p>

<section class="flex-section">
  <div class="text">
  <h2>üîç What are immune digital twins?</h2>
  <p>
    <strong>Immune digital twins</strong> are dynamic computational models designed to simulate how an individual‚Äôs immune system responds to infections, therapies, and vaccines. By integrating biological knowledge, clinical data, and modeling techniques, we aim to support:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Personalized diagnostics</li>
    <li>Predictive medicine</li>
    <li>Targeted therapeutic development</li>
    <li>Systems-level understanding of immunity</li>
  </ul>
