*18 Nov 2025, 8:00 am – 5:00 pm UTC, Paris - France*

<a target="_blank" href="https://www.rd-alliance.org/events/rda-europe-annual-summit/">https://www.rd-alliance.org/events/rda-europe-annual-summit/</a>

<p>The <b>first RDA in Europe Annual Summit</b> will take place on <b>18 November 2025</b> in Paris.
The event is an RDA Europe initiative, co-organised and hosted by <b>RDA France</b> and <b>CNRS</b>, and will be co-located with the Annual RDA France meeting on <b>17 November</b>.</p>
<p>Taking place at <i>CNRS, 3 Rue Michel Ange, 75016 Paris, Auditorium Marie-Curie</i>, the event will provide a dedicated forum for current and prospective European RDA regions and their members to address challenges and solutions at both national and pan-European levels, and discuss RDA’s role and priorities within the broader European landscape.</p>
<p>It will also offer an opportunity to address key topics relevant to the European RDA community, through the lens of the RDA and to advance its impact across Europe.</p>
<p>Our chair, Anna Niarakis, will be presenting the achievements of our BIDT WG.</p>
